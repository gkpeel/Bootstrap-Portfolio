About Me

Trying to give you the quick-and-dirty about me, while still making it quick is a challenge.

I’ve lived all over - New Jersey, Gainesville, Savannah, Las Vegas, Miami, Los Angeles, Cape Canaveral, and currently Philly. I plan to live a bunch more places. (I see you Peru.)

I’ve held a number of jobs. Having worked in the film industry - as a freelance Producer/AD, an on-set PA, an office intern, or an executive assistant, I’ve seen how the proverbial “sausage is made” and decided it wasn’t for me.

Since leaving the west coast and returning to the best coast, I’ve worked as a freelance inbound marketing consultant, digital project manager, and WordPress developer.
